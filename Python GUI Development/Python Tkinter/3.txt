Prime minister Narendra Modi on Saturday evening inaugurated the 11th Khel Mahakumbh after he held a roadshow on Riverfront Road in Ahmedabad. Addressing the sportsperson, Modi said that he has faith in the young players, their dedication, adding, "I have trust on sankalp, samarpan of our players. I can say with confidence that our youth power would take it much further. We would soon be among countries winning many golds."
